Pon Klie Zen Qigong

Hae Kwang (Robert) Sunim will be teaching "Soft' Pon Klie Qi-Gong and Meditation also at WELLSPRING, a network of cancer support centres. Wellspring programs are free of charge and open to individuals and caregivers who are coping with any type of cancer, at any stage. In addition to improved fintness and flexibility, Pon Klie Zen Qi-gong helps the body and and mind release built up stress and tension. Each class includes core body exercises, Yoga, Taichi and Meditation. The class is further enhanced with key Life Force Energy. First Class is free, just show up.
