Wellcome!

Bo Kwang Qi Gong and Meditation Centre provides Zen meditation, Qi Gong instruction, Mindfulness and Inner peace training as well as educational programs within a kind and supportive environment. We have classes and programs in Toronto, Brampton, Oakville, Burlington and Kitchener, Ontario. We offer daily practice schedule and Dharma talks with our guiding teacher, Hae Kwang Robert (Sunim). Our goal is to reach people and share Zen, Qi Gong, hope, healing and love to many that are struggling. Come join our growing community.
