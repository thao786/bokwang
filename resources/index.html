Wellcome!

Bo Kwang Qi Gong and Meditation Centre provides Zen meditation and Qi Gong instruction as well as Mindfulness and Inner peace training and educational programs all within a kind and supportive environment. We have classes and programs in Toronto, Brampton, Oakville, Burlington and Kitchener, Ontario. We offer an almost daily practice schedule as well as Dharma talks with our guiding teacher, Hae Kwang Robert (Sunim). Our goal is to reach people and share Zen, Qi Gong, hope, healing, and love to many that are struggling. Come join our growing community.

Inner Peace Qi Gong

Qi-Gong is a popular form of complimentary mind-body medicine that strengthens and enhances energy and calms the mind. Qigong is translated from the Chinese to mean "energy cultivation" or "working with the life force energy." Pon Klie Zen Qigong (the form that we teach) specializes in re-harmonizing mind-body and deeply resting the nervous system. Hae Kwang Sunim states that “this form of Qigong works in exactly the opposite way that stress does. It deeply rests the nervous system and restores the body to a calm state. It helps the body to repair itself and it improves the flow of Qi (energy) while reducing the physical effects of stress.” As a result, “heart rate and breathing slow down, blood pressure improves, circulation and flexibility is enhanced and oxygen is used more efficiently. The body ‘let’s go’ and immune function improves.” Similar to Reiki and Therapeutic Touch, Qi-Gong also teaches practitioners to “project into and balance the energy in their body. It restores the yin-yang balance and helps the body build reserve”.
