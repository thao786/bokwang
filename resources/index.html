Wellcome!

Bo Kwang Zen Center provides Zen meditation and Qi Gong instruction in Toronto, Canada within a kind and supportive environment. We offer a almost daily practice schedule as well as Dharma talks with our guiding teacher, Zen Master Hae Kwang Robert. Our goal is to reach people and share Zen, Qi Gong, hope, healing, and love to many that are struggling.
