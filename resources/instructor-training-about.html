About Pon Klie Zen Qigong(P.K.Z.Q.) Standing Series

In the Western world, exercise programs focus mainly just on strength and fitness. In the East, exercise systems incorporate fitness practices for the body, mind, and spirit. This important balance is strongly emphasized in our Pon Klie (Bad Stress Out) Zen Qigong. It focuses on releasing tension and energy blockages through the whole body, opening Sen Lines and restoring peace and inner harmony. It strengthens the mind-body connection and harmonizes the bodies "Chi" (life force energy) as one moves through the flow.

The P.K.Z.Q. movements also increase strength, bone density and flexibility through tendon stretching. The deep breathing and energy tapping in the flow further support the body by helping to release toxins.

There is a great need for Pon Klie Zen Qigong Instructors today. Stress is a big issue and Qigong helps restore and harmonize mind and body at the root level.

Also, as we were told by the manager at a large city recreation centre in the area, “We have files full of applications from yoga teachers wanting to work at our centre. That is not what we need. We are looking for something new. Something that even more people can participate in. Qigong is what we were looking for!”
