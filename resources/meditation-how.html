Zazen Meditation for the brave and true

"Look Within."

- Thomas Edison -

Zazen meditation is to observe the mind. The priciple is to observe everything presently unfolding in your thoughts and sensations. Your job is to be one step above your thought, observe it but not entangle with it. Let the thought move through you.

Zazen meditation could be exciting as the mind would throw the craziest imaginations or dig up long gone memories in order to pull you out of focus. Your job is to see it, and not touch it. Just sit back and watch the movie.
