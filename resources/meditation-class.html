Meditation classes and groups

At Bo Kwang Centre we offer meditation classes for beginner starting every 2nd Thursday every month. In addition to that we held meditation group night every week. Everyone is welcome to join us.

Come join us for meditation group. We're all on the same path. You are not alone.

Participant Notice:

Please note that some tea circles / group talks / event and group answer sessions with Hae Kwang Sunim (Robert) are recorded or skyped out as a possible teaching tool. If any participant wishes not to be recorded please let us know.

For more information and registration please refer to our meetup page.
