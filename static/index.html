Welcome!

Mind, Body & Spirit: Bo Kwang Zen, Qi Gong and Meditation Centre is a place where people come together and support each in the practice of Zen, Mindfulness, Meditation, Inner Peace Qigong and the Healing Arts. Our classes run regularly in Brampton, Oakville, Thornhill, Richmond Hill and Toronto, Ontario. We welcome people of all faiths and those who do not subscribe to any faith. We offer meditation and supportive training at all levels, from beginner to advanced. You will find a warm, friendly community here. Come join our growing community.
We are here to share with everyone, regardless of background, experience or belief.

Authentic Zen Order
We are a part of one of the largest Zen orders in the world with over 3,100 Zen Meditation Centres and the beautiful (historical) Seonamsa Zen Temple in S. Korea (built in 529 C.E.). For more of our classes please click here
